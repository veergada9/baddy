Badminton

Let's have a brief Information about Badminton.

Badminton is a racquet sport played using racquets to hit a shuttlecock across a net.

Types of Formats

There are several types of formats like:

  • Singles: The game is 1 vs 1
  • Doubles: The game is 2 vs 2
